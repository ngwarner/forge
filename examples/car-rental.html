Money-saving tips
📅Book at least 1–2 weeks ahead — last-minute rentals are almost always more expensive.
🏪Pick up off-airport — airport locations add a 20–30% surcharge. A nearby Enterprise or Hertz often saves significantly.
🛒Check Costco Travel and Sam's Club — member rates frequently beat everything else.
💳Your credit card may include free collision coverage — check before paying for rental insurance.
🔄Search for free cancellation rates, then rebook closer to your trip if prices drop.
⛽Always return the car with a full tank — prepaid fuel options are almost never worth it.
👤If the driver is under 25, ask about young driver fees — they vary a lot between companies.
